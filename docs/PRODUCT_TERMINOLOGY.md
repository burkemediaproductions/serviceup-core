# ServiceUp product terminology

ServiceUp's themed names have precise product meanings while remaining understandable through subtitles and supporting copy.

| Name | Product meaning | Mascot inspiration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Widgets | Small cards, summaries, and actions on the ServiceUp dashboard | Widget |
| Gizmos | Capabilities, integrations, and backend extensions | Gizmo |
| Gadgets | Installable solutions, module packs, websites, or app configurations | Gadget |
| Pixels | Images, videos, documents, and other managed media assets | Pixel (Zelda) |

Pixel is a small Shih Tzu/pug mix and the physically largest/chubbiest member of the pet group—the visual joke is that a pixel is the smallest display unit. Pixel is an equal member of the mascot family, not the primary ServiceUp mascot.

General CRM and CMS concepts retain conventional labels such as Clients, Contacts, Content, Content Types, Entries, Pages, and Settings. New themed terms should only be introduced when a durable product concept genuinely needs one.

**Profile** is intentionally conventional. It opens the Organization Profile used for reusable names, descriptions, phone numbers, email addresses, locations, websites, and social profiles.

Headers and Footers are frontend components rather than primary dashboard sections. Their editable values come from Menus, Profile, Branding, Pixels, Site Settings, and Content. Legacy routes remain available for compatibility while existing configurations are migrated.
